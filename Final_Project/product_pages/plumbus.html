Plumbus

Product Description

A Plumbus is an all purpose home device. Everyone knows what it does, so there is no reason to explain it. It was first shown in the episode, "Interdimensional Cable 2: Tempting Fate" where it was seen twice on the Interdimensional TV First when Stealy stole one from a workplace and made a sale offer for it, and again in an episode of "How They Do It" that shows the viewers how plumbuses are made. They are mentioned as being a common household and business place item used as an accent piece to the room.

Plumbus or Plumbuses are worth six-and-a-half Brapples according to Stealy.
